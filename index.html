About Me

I am a PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB). Prior to joining UCSB, I earned my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering with a minor in Mathematics from Sharif University of Technology in 2023. I am currently an intern at Handshake AI, focusing on improving and evaluating LLM-generated code experiments.

My research interests span text-based and multimodal large language models (LLMs), with emphasis on:

  • Personalization of LLMs and VLMs
  • Efficient fine-tuning of LLMs and VLMs
  • Federated and decentralized learning
  • Robust machine learning

Feel free to reach out if you're interested in discussing a collaboration!
